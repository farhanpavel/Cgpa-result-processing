Guide

1. Overview

The CGPA result processing system is a web application designed to help educational institutions to process academic results. The system enables educational institutions to easily input and process academic data, including Students marks and generate CGPA results for individual students. Along with Index page, it has two main module:

  • Login and Registration panel
  • Result Panel

The main function of this project are given below-

  1. Aims to develop a CGPA calculation program.
  2. Accepts incourse marks and examiner marks.
  3. Converts marks to grade points based on a standard grading scale.
  4. Calculate Grade Point for each course .
  5. Adds together the multiple of credit and grade point for all course . Calculates the CGPA by dividing the total product by the total credit hours

2. CGPA Calculation

Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is a metric used to measure a student's overall academic performance in a particular program. It is calculated by taking the average of the grade points obtained by a student in all the courses taken during a semester, and is expressed on a scale of 0-4. The grade points are calculated by assigning numerical values to the letter grades obtained by the student in each course, according to a predetermined grading system. The numerical values are then multiplied by the course credits to obtain the grade point for each course. The CGPA is then calculated by dividing the total grade points obtained by the total number of credits taken by the student. CGPA is an important metric used by educational institutions to assess a student's academic performance, and it is often used by employers to evaluate a candidate's suitability for a job. An automated CGPA calculation system can simplify the grading process, reduce the workload of educators, and ensure accurate and reliable grading of students, while providing real-time updates and reports to stakeholders.
